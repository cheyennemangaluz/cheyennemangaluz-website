ABOUT ME





PERSONAL INFORMATION

Hi! My name is Cheyenne C. Mangaluz, and I am 18 years old. I was born on January 8, 2006. My zodiac sign is Capricorn. I lived in Salangan, San Miguel, Bulacan. I am the eldest child, and I have two brothers. My parents' names are Alvin D. Mangaluz and Mary Ann C. Mangaluz. Everyone calls me by my nicknames, Yen, Che, and Chey. I am kind, reliable, and friendly. My life's motto is "Feel the fear and do it anyway". And my message to the little me is: I hope she's proud of the person I have become, and someday I'll make her proud.


THINGS I LIKE

I like blue and pink colors, and I like spending time with my family by watching series and movies. When I'm bored, I like to listen to music and podcasts, read books, look at paintings on the internet, watch sunrises, go on spontaneous trips and nature trips with my family, watch Disney movies because I like the feeling of being a child again, go out after school with my funny friends, and explore new things for new memories.
